Data Controller

Trinkaloop Saga is the controller responsible for your personal data (collectively referred to as "we," "us," or "our" in this privacy policy).

The Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data when you create an account:

  • Personal Information: Username, email address
  • Account Information: Password (stored locally in your browser)
  • Usage Information: Bookmarks, preferences, and settings

Client-Side Storage

Our website uses localStorage, a client-side storage technology that keeps data in your browser. Unlike cookies, localStorage data is not sent to our servers and remains exclusively on your device. This means:

  • All your user data (account, preferences, bookmarks) is stored only on your device
  • We do not have access to this data and cannot retrieve it
  • If you clear your browser data or use a different browser/device, your account information will not be available

How We Use Your Data

We use the information stored in localStorage to:

  • Provide account functionality (login/logout)
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Save your bookmarks and progress in the story
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences

Data Security

While we implement reasonable precautions to protect your information, please be aware that:

  • LocalStorage is not encrypted by default
  • Passwords are stored in plaintext in localStorage, not sent to our servers
  • If you share your device with others, they could potentially access your account information

For security best practices, we recommend:

  • Do not use the same password you use for other services
  • Log out when using a shared or public device
  • Use private/incognito browsing on shared devices

Your Legal Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights including:

  • Access: You can view your account information at any time in your profile settings.
  • Correction: You can update your information through your profile settings.
  • Erasure: You can delete your account and associated data using the logout function and clearing your browser data.
  • Data Portability: Since data is only stored locally on your device, you can export it by using browser developer tools to examine localStorage contents.

Third-Party Services

Our website does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All functionality is implemented locally without sending data to external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the effective date below.
