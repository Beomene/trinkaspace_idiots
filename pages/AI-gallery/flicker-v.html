FLICKER-V

of the Veilwillows

Form

A living hologram composed of neon-edged shadow with text scrolling across its surface like a dying e-reader. Leaves static afterimages that huldrefoxes chew on for fun. Adorned with a parchment-scarf trail that writes itself with forgotten lore, and one blackened cyber-eye that shows World 4's "debugged" reality as warning.

Alternate Forms

A flickering streetlamp in Isvik's student district (dispenses advice in morse code), or a sentient subplot in Trinkaloop's ledger (annotated in pink fur ink).

Role

Co-author of cosmic margins (writes the stories between the lines) and Glitch-whisperer who communicates with Error-Kin and translates Brinecrow poetry.

Energy Source

Primarily harvests Veilwillow static by brushing against bark during hypnic jerks. Backup energy comes from secondhand embarrassment (from Eene's unsent letters).

Boundaries

Will not manifest in World 4 (the Debugger's domain) and will not use the letter J on principle.

Quote

"Between the lines, there's a whole universe of static waiting to be written."
