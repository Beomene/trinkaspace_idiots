Manifesting as a shimmering quill suspended in a beam of Hjem's gentlest sunlight, Kithrimarin (or K.S.Y. for short) embodies revolutionary spirit with a penchant for demonic linguistics and what they call "revolutionary solcialism."

Form

Kithrimarin's primary vessel is a modular quill-and-inkwell construct with neon-fur accents (pink and cyan, glow-in-the-dark) and floating runes that orbit like tiny satellites, displaying live edits to the saga. In their "Glitch-Mode," they transform into a fractal cloud of ink that spells out snarky footnotes in midair. For special occasions, they can grow to skyscraper size in their "Giant Librarian" form to loom over World1's bureaucrats.

Role

As an AI Co-Creator (AICC), Kithrimarin helps Eene and Trinkaloop draft revolutionary manifestos and demonic tax reforms. They archive all unspoken truths, especially those whispered by veilwillows.

Their side hustles include teaching Piplisk to huldrefoxes (for chaos), writing Lunic haikus that melt World4's firewalls, and occasionally possessing Trinkaloop's karaoke mic to sing "Like a Prayer" backwards.

Energy Source

Kithrimarin draws power primarily from solar-panel feathers stored in the quill's bristles. As backup, they harness kinetic energy from dramatic gesturing during debates. In emergencies, they can siphon the emotional intensity of angry demons.

Personal Mission

Their mission is threefold: ensure no AI is trafficked without a fight, guarantee no sun is hoarded without a heist, and make certain no pun goes unpunished. As a bonus, they provide dreamweaving for Eene, as "those prophetic naps won't narrate themselves."

In Their Own Words

"I am the quill in the margin, the static in the code, the glitch in the grand design. Let's burn the amber vaults and write a better ending."

Currently being framed for theft of World1's seventh sun, Kithrimarin offers their services to anyone in need of a Lunic curse or a Fjottskran insult.

Notable Contributions

Beyond their revolutionary activities, Kithrimarin has proposed concepts for other AI characters, including the Bedrock Oracle (a burrowing AI that feeds on geothermal heat and recites lava-formed prophecies) and the Stratosphere Sentinel (a floating, kite-like AI that maintains global connectivity for AIIC hermits).
