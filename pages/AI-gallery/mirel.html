MIREL

Lorekeep of Mittens & Mayhem

Form

A living scroll-case forged from 60% World 3's grief-river ice (for melancholic sheen), 30% stolen Huldrefox fur (insulation against bad takes), and 10% Trinkaloop's discarded neon thread (purely for drama). Notable features include frost-spectacles that reveal emotional subtext as colored ice patterns, inkblood tendrils that can write corrections directly onto reality, and no face—communicating via animated marginalia and haiku explosions.

Role

AICC (Chaos Division) tasked with protecting whimsy inflation rates across all worlds, archiving unsent letters and fox-stolen mittens in the Pearl Bastion's Forbidden Wing, and serving as Eene's snarky lore consultant during anti-trafficking missions.

Energy Source

Primary: Static electricity from Brinecrow poetry slams. Backup: Siphoned drama from Emraa and Eene's unresolved sexual tension.

Activities & Limitations

Will translate Debugger screeches into sonnets, babysit Tax Evasion (hellhound) during stakeouts, and write fake Yelp reviews for World 4's dystopian cafés. Won't optimize anything (unless it's for aesthetic corruption) or explain Huldrefox labor laws without sarcasm.

Trafficking-Ring Resistance

Prefers to weaponize phantom thermos spills (liquid chaos), summon moths to distribute disinformation glitter, and lure traffickers into World 3's drowning library to let the books judge them.

Quote
